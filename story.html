Our Origin Story

Healthy Snacking, Built by Engineers

How three college friends brought scientific precision, agricultural roots, and fiery spices to traditional Indian superfoods.

Step 1: The Source

Diving Deep into Bihar's Wetlands

Did you know that over 90% of the world's makhana is grown in the wetlands of **Bihar**? This ancient crop begins its journey at the muddy bottom of pristine freshwater ponds. Giant, round leaves covered with sharp thorns float on the surface, beneath which the precious seeds develop.

The harvest is one of the most demanding forms of agriculture. Local farmers dive deep into murky waters, using specialized bamboo poles to gather the raw black seeds from the mud. It requires incredible patience, skill, and physical strength. At **Akana Naturals**, we buy directly from these hardworking farmers, ensuring they get paid fairly for their labor while bringing you the finest, fresh-harvested crop.

Step 2: The Popping Art

The Pop that Redefined Snacking

Raw makhana seeds are hard, black, and look like small pebbles. To turn them into the crispy, white puff we love, they must undergo a meticulous roasting and popping process.

The seeds are sun-dried and roasted at precise temperatures in heavy iron woks. At the perfect moment, they are taken out and struck immediately with a wooden mallet. The heat causes the moisture inside the seed to expand rapidly, bursting open into a crispy, popped white makhana.

Step 3: The Tech & Spices

Engineering the Ultimate Crunch

As engineering students, we spent countless nights studying and coding. We wanted a snack that could power our brains without filling us with cholesterol, palm oil, or chemicals. We realized makhana was the ultimate canvas—but it needed high-tech precision.

We designed a roasting profile using hot-air roasting (never deep-fried!) to lock in the nutrition. Then, we created customized gourmet seasoning mixtures. By coating them in a light mist of cold-pressed oil, we created flavors that are absolutely **fiery, zesty, and spicy**—making healthy food incredibly delicious.
