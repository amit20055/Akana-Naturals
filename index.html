Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about shipping, shelf-life, or sourcing? We have answers.

How is Akana Makhana roasted?

We dry-roast our makhana at controlled temperatures to preserve nutrition. We do not deep fry. A minor mist of olive oil is used solely to allow spices to coat each seed uniformly.

What is the shelf life of the product?

Our bags are nitrogen-flushed and sealed. Unopened, they remain fresh for up to 6 months. Once opened, store in an airtight container to maintain crispness.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes, we offer free shipping across India for all orders. There is no minimum cart value needed to qualify for free delivery.

Is it safe for toddlers and pregnant women?

Yes! Fox nuts are fully natural seeds rich in calcium and iron, making them an excellent healthy nutrient source for pregnant women and toddlers. We recommend our Classic Salted flavor for young kids.
